# Resilient Rides Brisbane
## Straightforward mobile bike service for the inner east

![The Resilient Rides mobile workshop](assets/images/hero-workshop.jpg)

**Reliable mechanical care that comes to your door.** Resilient Rides provides honest servicing, e-bike diagnostics, and refurbished bikes for commuters who value their time and their agency. We solve the logistics gap: no shopfront drop-offs, no traffic, and no hauling heavy bikes across town. Just expert service delivered by bike.

### Our philosophy: why we ride

Bicycles are the ultimate tool for personal agency. In an increasingly complex world, a well-maintained bike is a simple, effective hedge against fragility. It improves your health, costs next to nothing to run, and keeps you moving regardless of the state of the broader economy.

Resilient Rides was started to bridge the gap between professional mechanical care and the reality of inner-city living. We know that getting a non-functional bike—especially a heavy e-bike—to a workshop is a major hurdle. We’ve traded the traditional retail space for a heavy-duty e-bike workshop so we can provide expert service exactly where you need it.

By operating entirely within a 7km radius of East Brisbane, we keep our footprint small and our focus local. Whether you're a daily commuter or looking for a second-hand bike with soul, we’re here to keep your wheels turning and your transport resilient.

### The Second Life program

**Refurbished bikes with soul**
We believe the most sustainable bike is the one already built. Resilient Rides sources high-quality steel and alloy frames that have been sidelined by the disposable bike industry. We restore them to peak mechanical condition, replacing worn parts with reliable, modern components.

![A refurbished Second Life icon](assets/images/refurbished-bike.jpg)

These aren't just used bikes; they are **Second Life** icons—resilient, repairable, and built to be ridden every day. 

* **The vibe:** Better than a cheap new bike; more character than a generic one.
* **The guarantee:** Every refurbished bike comes with a 3-month mechanical guarantee and a complimentary 6-week safety check.

### Service area

**Where we ride**
Resilient Rides operates within a 7km radius of **East Brisbane**. By using a heavy-duty e-bike workshop instead of a van, we avoid traffic and parking delays, ensuring we arrive on time.

We service the following inner-city hubs:
* **Inner East:** East Brisbane, Kangaroo Point, Norman Park, Bulimba, Coorparoo.
* **Inner North/South:** New Farm, South Brisbane, Woolloongabba.
* **Inner West:** West End, Highgate Hill, Toowong, Milton.
